Strawberry Frosted Donuts are the Best!

If you go somewhere to get donuts, this is why you should get a strawberry frosted donut...

Strawberry Donuts are very delicious. Everytime I go to Dunkin I get a strawberry frosted donut. If you like strawberry flavoring, these are perfect for you!

Strawberry Frosted Donuts have a pink frosting with rainbow sprinkles on top!

When you bite into a strawberry frosted donut, its amazing. It is sweet and moist. They are only a dollar at Dunkin Donuts! A Strawberry Frosted is always the way to go, no matter what mood you are in.
